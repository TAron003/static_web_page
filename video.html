Bevezető

A PJO, azaz a Percy Jackson és az Olimposziak a Riordanverse első könyvsorozata, Rick Riordan lehengerlő és magával ragadó stílusában mutatja be Percy Jackson és két társa, Annabeth Chase és Grover Underwood kalandjait, illetve a görög mitológia, az istenek, szörnyek és félistenek világát napjainkba átültetve.

Young Percy talking with her mother.
Percy Jackson: Perseus. That's me.
Percy and Sally glance upon a statue of the greek hero Perseus inside The Met.
Sally Jackson (Percy's mom): Mm-hm. That's who you're named after.
Percy: Because he was a hero?
Sally: Because he was brave and kind, and, against all odds, he managed to find his way to a happy ending.

Disney+ logo comes in frame, lightning crackles over the letters.
Percy and Grover sit on the edge of a fountain.

Nancy Bobofit throws a sandwich at Grover.

Percy with widened eyes, shock and disbelief in his expression.

Sally: You're a half-blood.
Clarisse and some other Ares kids shoves Percy.
Sally: And half-bloods are not safe in the world.
